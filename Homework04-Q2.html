Correlation Matrix¶

The purpose of the correlation matrix is to understand how does variables are related to each other in our data set. In our dataset, there data for every given eventID (row). Elements of a column are belong to a distinct eventID. So, columns represent the distinct properties of an event. Some examples of the columns are: Mass of the hadronic channels, azimuthal angle, pseudorapidity, etc.
